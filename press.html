Test Your French is a privacy-first suite of lightweight French assessment games designed to help English speakers avoid real-world language traps — no accounts, no ads, no tracking.

The origin

The project was born from a simple observation: many English speakers underestimate how deceptive French can be — especially false friends that look familiar but mislead in real life.

A word like actuellement looks safe. It isn’t. Those small misunderstandings can create real friction — in exams, in paperwork, at work, or in daily life in France.

Test Your French focuses on those blind spots.

Word Traps

A fast-paced vocabulary game about French-English false friends and misleading similarities.

Players see a French word that resembles English and must decide: same meaning — or trap?

Each run gives 3 chances. Instant feedback includes short, pedagogically focused explanations. A premium Practice mode allows players to review and fix recurring mistakes.

Play: wordtraps.com

Daily French

Short themed quizzes built around everyday life in France — ordering at a café, understanding a lease, navigating administrative appointments.

Includes native French audio and immediate feedback.

Play: app.testyourfrench.com

About the creator

Test Your French is an independent project created by Carole Stromboni, a French native speaker and founder of Test Your French. The games are built with a focus on clarity, accessibility, and respect for learners — no dark patterns, no manipulative gamification.
